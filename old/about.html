關於我們 / About Us

我們專注於家庭與電動船艇的智慧電池系統設計與製造，致力於打造穩定、潔淨且未來導向的能源解決方案。從家中到海上，全面實現電力自主。

We are a team of innovators and energy specialists driven to reshape the future of clean power. Our mission is simple: disrupt the traditional energy landscape by delivering cutting‑edge home battery storage systems and extending that same intelligent power to the water with advanced electric boat solutions.

From modern homes to marine mobility, we design and manufacture reliable, sustainable energy systems that empower our customers to live more independently, more efficiently, and with zero compromise on performance or design.
